chaussette by athoune

Chaussette is a blackbox to enjoy the hype of socket communication in your browser. Socket.io does the job for the communication, Chaussette handles the events in your own language.
wowie zowie socks by Lori_NY

How ?

You just do plain old http communication. You are talking to a REST server, Chaussette talks to you in http.

For what?

  • You know who is connected.
  • You can ping back a user when a long action is finished.
  • Tchat like informations.
  • Everything you want to push on the client side.

You don't care about funky async technology, you want to keep your good old framework.

Why this name ?

In french, when you ear socket you write it soquette : petite chaussette (short socks).
